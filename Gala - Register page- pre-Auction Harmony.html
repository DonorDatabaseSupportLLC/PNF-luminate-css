This year marks the 40th annual Park Nicollet Foundation Spotlight Gala.  Join us for our Virtual 40th anniversary Spotlight Gala event on Saturday evening, October 3.

Our event is free to attend and will be packed with inspiring stories and special guests to raise critical support for children’s health programs. 

Park Nicollet is one of the largest providers of pediatric health care in our region, committed to helping families and children thrive and reach their full potential. Park Nicollet Foundation provides funding support for an array of children’s health programs that extend far into communities of high-need, providing no-fee health care services, immunizations, and more to thousands of mothers, babies, children and teenagers every year.
