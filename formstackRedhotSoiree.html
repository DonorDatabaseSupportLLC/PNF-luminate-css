Join us for a one-of-a-kind virtual celebration!
Friday, February 26, 2021 at 7 p.m.

Red Heart Soirée benefits cardiovascular research, education and patient care at the University of Minnesota.

We are excited to announce Jennifer Mayerle and Mike Augustyniak from WCCO Saturday Morning as our co-hosts!

Jennifer Mayerle                     Mike Augustyniak

This year enjoy the live event from the comfort of your home! You will enjoy an online auction, heartwarming patient stories, messages from some of our beloved physicians, a behind-the-scenes look at the newest innovations in patient care, and a fun and entertaining Zoom after-party!

   More Information   

Registered guests will be notified when the auction opens and receive exciting event updates to maximize your Red Heart Soirée experience.

There are three ways to participate!

  1. Register - Register for free or choose the VIP option to receive a celebration meal kit for two.
  2. Donate - Make a donation today to support life-saving cardiovascular work at the University.
  3. Online Auction - Bidding for our exciting online auction opens on Friday, February 19!

Additional details are available at www.redheartsoiree.umn.edu.
